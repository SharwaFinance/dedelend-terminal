FAQ

What is Unified Trading Terminal?
Unified Trading Terminal allows you to trade on GMX or GNS without switching between interfaces.
Does DeDeLend take any fees?
We take zero fees, so you'll pay the same fees if you trade on the original interface, GMX or GNS.
Is it safe?
We don't use any contracts. You interact directly with GMX's and GNS's contracts. We provide only an interface between you and the blockchain world.
What if I have a problem with Unified Trading Terminal?
If you're having any trouble with Unified Trading Terminal, you can always manage your positions using the original GMX or GNS interfaces to handle your positions.
