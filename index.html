FAQ

What is DeDeLend?
DeDeLend is an options aggregator that offers a wide range of instruments. Users can effortlessly construct any option strategy and purchase it in a single transaction, as well as set up take-profit or stop-loss orders.
Is it safe?
Our contracts have not been audited. As DeDeLend is currently in its beta phase, we ask that you keep this in mind. We cannot guarantee refunds in the event of lost funds, but if you encounter any issues, please do not hesitate to contact us via Discord or Twitter.
What if I have a problem with DeDeLend?
If you encounter any problems with DeDeLend, we encourage you to reach out to us through Discord or Twitter. We value your feedback and are here to assist you.
Does DeDeLend charge any fees?
No, DeDeLend does not charge any additional fees. You will pay the same price for options as you would on the original protocols.
